# transfer_confusion_adjudicated_5class

Per (variant, bracket) confusion matrices for the transferability experiment under the 5-class collapse, the collapsed counterpart to `reports/transfer_confusion_adjudicated/` (10-class). Generated by `scripts/build_transfer_confusion_5class.py`, reusing the reference selection and per-bracket prediction reading of `scripts/build_transfer_confusion.py` and the collapse of `scripts/collapsed_5class_confusion.py`.

## Inputs

- Predictions: `data/raw/transfer_predictions/<bracket>/pred_<bracket>_cell<id>.tif`, 5 bands (band1=v2 ... band5=v6), values 1 to 10, temporally matched per bracket.
- Reference: the adjudicated reviewer from truth_selections.csv (72 multi-interpreted cells), collapsed from CKIT codes to the 5-class scheme.

## Collapse

Scheme: Stable (all no-change classes folded, including Other) plus Harvest, Development, Insect/Disease, and Beaver. The reference is collapsed from CKIT codes to 1..5 (Other(13) to Stable, Unknown(10) and Fire(40) dropped), and the predictions from the 10-class schema to 1..5. This differs from the 10-class reference crosswalk, which drops Other(13) rather than folding it into Stable, so these collapsed matrices are the authority for the 5-class view, not a re-aggregation of the 10-class counts.

## Disjoint-cell caveat

The five brackets use disjoint cell sets (36 cells each, no cell shared across brackets), so a bracket-to-bracket difference confounds temporal transfer with the differing cell composition and landscape difficulty. These are five independent assessments, not a controlled transfer curve. 2018_2020 is the in-sample control, scored on its own 36 cells.

## Support and low-support classes

At 36 cells per bracket the change classes have few reference pixels. `transfer_metrics_long.csv` carries `support` (reference pixel count per class) and `cells_present` (cells containing the class); `low_support` fires when a class appears in fewer than 5 cells or has under 100 px. Low-support classes per bracket (v2, representative):

- 2017_2019: none flagged
- 2018_2020: none flagged
- 2019_2021: none flagged
- 2020_2022: none flagged
- 2021_2023: Insect/Disease (3768 px, 4 cells)

## Outputs

- `cm_<variant>_<bracket>.csv`: 25 matrices (5 variants x 5 brackets), 5x5 raw counts, reference on rows (diagonal is producer's accuracy), prediction on columns.
- `cm_<variant>_<bracket>.png`: count heatmaps with PA/UA margins and OA/kappa.
- `transfer_metrics_long.csv`: one row per (variant, bracket, collapsed class) with per-class precision, recall, F1, IoU, support, cells_present, and low_support, plus the aggregate OA, all-Stable baseline, macro-F1, mean IoU, and kappa, and a `control` flag (true for 2018_2020).
