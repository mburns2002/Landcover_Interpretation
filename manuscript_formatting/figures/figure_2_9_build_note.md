Cell 31320 (bracket 2018_2020) was used. Its footprint (464760, 2593250, 468130, 2596620 in EPSG:5070)
equals the earlier crop bounds exactly, so the location is reused for continuity. It contains a water
body that is contiguous under v2 to v5 and dissolves into speckle under v6.

current per-bracket classifications and the earlier static-mosaic classifications are different maps on
different footprints.
